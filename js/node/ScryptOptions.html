typedef ScryptOptions

package js.node

import js.node.Crypto

Options for Crypto.scrypt and Crypto.scryptSync.

Fields

@:optionaloptionalr:Null<Int>

Alias for blockSize. Only one of blockSize or r may be specified.

@:optionaloptionalparallelization:Null<Int>

Parallelization parameter. Default: 1. Only one of parallelization or p may be specified.

@:optionaloptionalp:Null<Int>

Alias for parallelization. Only one of parallelization or p may be specified.

@:optionaloptionalmaxmem:Null<Int>

Memory upper bound. It is an error when (approximately) 128 * N * r > maxmem. Default: 32 * 1024 * 1024.

@:optionaloptionalcost:Null<Int>

CPU/memory cost parameter. Must be a power of two greater than one. Default: 16384. Only one of cost or N may be specified.

@:optionaloptionalblockSize:Null<Int>

Block size parameter. Default: 8. Only one of blockSize or r may be specified.

@:optionaloptionalN:Null<Int>

Alias for cost. Only one of cost or N may be specified.
